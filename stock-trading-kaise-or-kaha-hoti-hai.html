Introduction

Agar aap pehli baar Share Market ke baare mein seekh rahe hain, to aapke dimaag mein ye sawal zarur aata hoga ki Stocks mein Trading kaise aur kahan hoti hai?

Is article mein hum beginner-friendly language mein step by step samjhenge ki Trading kya hoti hai, Trading kahan hoti hai, account kaise khulta hai aur Trading shuru karne ke liye kin cheezon ki zarurat hoti hai.

Agar aapne kabhi Trading nahi ki hai, to chinta ki koi baat nahi. Ye guide specially beginners ke liye likhi gayi hai.

Trading Kya Hoti Hai?

Trading ka matlab hota hai kisi company ke shares ko Buy (Kharidna) aur Sell (Bechna), taaki price badhne ya ghatne se profit kamaya ja sake.

Simple shabdon mein, agar aap kisi company ka share ₹100 mein kharidte hain aur baad mein uski price ₹110 ho jaati hai, to aap use bechkar profit kama sakte hain.

  • ✅ Pehle Trading ke basics seekhen.
  • ✅ Chhoti amount se practice shuru karein.
  • ✅ Hamesha Risk Management follow karein.
  • ✅ Emotional hokar trade na karein.
  • ✅ Trading Journal maintain karein.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Kya Trading Mobile Se Ki Ja Sakti Hai?

Haan. Aaj lagbhag sabhi brokers Android aur iPhone ke liye mobile app provide karte hain.

2. Trading Shuru Karne Ke Liye Kitne Paise Chahiye?

Aap chhoti amount se bhi shuruaat kar sakte hain. Zaroori amount is baat par depend karti hai ki aap kaunsa share kharidna chahte hain.

3. Kya Demat Aur Trading Account Alag-Alag Banana Padta Hai?

Nahi. Aam taur par ek trusted broker ke saath account open karne par dono accounts ek hi process mein khul jaate hain.

4. Kya Trading Risky Hai?

Haan. Agar bina knowledge aur bina Risk Management ke trading ki jaye to nuksan ho sakta hai.
