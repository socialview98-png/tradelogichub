Trading Kya Hoti Hai?

Trading ka simple meaning hai kisi financial asset ko buy aur sell karna. Lekin beginner ke liye sabse important cheez “jaldi profit” nahi, balki process, risk aur discipline samajhna hai.

Trading ko simple example se samjho

Maan lo kisi company ka share ₹100 par available hai. Aap analysis karke sochte hain ki price future mein badh sakta hai. Aap share buy karte hain. Agar aap baad mein usse higher price par sell karte hain, to profit ho sakta hai. Agar price neeche chala jaye, to loss bhi ho sakta hai.

Isliye trading mein sirf direction guess karna enough nahi hota. Entry, exit aur risk ka plan pehle banana padta hai.

Trading mein kya buy aur sell hota hai?

  • Stocks: Companies ke shares.
  • Indices: Market ka group, jaise India mein Nifty 50 aur Sensex; globally S&P 500 jaise indices.
  • Commodities: Gold, silver ya crude oil jaise products.
  • Currencies: Different countries ki currencies, jaise USD/INR, EUR/USD aur GBP/USD currency pairs.
  • Crypto assets: Digital assets, jaise Bitcoin (BTC) aur Ethereum (ETH).

Har market ka risk aur rules alag hote hain. Beginner ko pehle basics aur ek market ko achchhe se samajhna chahiye.

Trading aur investing mein farq

Investing usually long term hoti hai—months ya years ke liye. Trading comparatively shorter time frame mein buy/sell par focus karti hai—days, weeks, ya kabhi intraday. Dono mein risk hota hai, lekin trading mein decisions aur risk control zyada frequent hote hain.

Beginner ko kaise start karna chahiye?

  1. Basics seekho: Candlesticks, support-resistance aur risk management samjho.
  2. Paper trading karo: Real money se pehle practice aur observation karo.
  3. Plan likho: Entry, stop loss aur target ka basic plan banao.
  4. Small risk rakho: Ek trade mein poora capital risk mat karo.
  5. Journal banao: Har trade se kya seekha, note karo.

Sabse common beginner mistake

Beginners aksar bina plan ke trade lete hain, loss ke baad position badha dete hain, ya social media tips par blindly depend karte hain. Better approach yeh hai: har decision ka reason samjho aur loss ko trading ka normal part maano.

Final thought

Trading ek skill hai, shortcut nahi. Agar aap learning, patience aur risk management par focus karoge, to aapka decision-making process gradually better hoga.
